Preparation
What to Review Before Each Exam
  • ✦Know the definition of each part of speech covered in the session
  • ✦Practice identifying parts of speech in simple sentences
  • ✦Review example sentences from class (especially Islamic context examples)
  • ✦Practice writing your own sentences for each part of speech
  • ✦For the Final Exam: review all 8 parts of speech and how they work together in a paragraph
  • ✦Review your worksheet answers and any feedback from the teacher
✦
Advice
Exam Tips
Read carefully Read each question fully before answering. Don't rush — the exam is not timed by section.
Start with what you know Answer the questions you're confident about first, then return to harder ones.
Use your examples If you forget a rule, think of the example sentences from class (Islamic ones are fine!).
Say Bismillah Start your exam with Bismillah and trust in your preparation. You've worked hard!
For writing sections Write naturally — short, clear sentences are better than long, confusing ones.
No grade is the end If you need a remedial session, that's perfectly okay. Growth is what matters most.

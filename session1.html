Meeting Breakdown
1

Welcome & Introduction to Parts of Speech

Overview · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students understand what "parts of speech" means and why it matters in English.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: Introduce yourself in English (name, where you're from)
10 – 20 min
Icebreaker: "How many English words do you know?" — group activity
20 – 40 min
Lesson: What are Parts of Speech? Overview of all 8 types with simple Islamic examples
40 – 55 min
Activity: Sort word cards into categories (guess the part of speech)
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap + preview of next meeting
Overview Key vocab: word, sentence, grammar, part of speech 🌙 Use: masjid, hijab, salah, Quran as example nouns
2

Nouns

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can identify and use nouns (person, place, thing, idea).
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: "Name 5 things you see at home"
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is a Noun? Types: common, proper, concrete, abstract
30 – 45 min
Activity: Noun hunt — find nouns in short Islamic sentences
45 – 55 min
Practice: Fill in the blank with the correct noun
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap + homework preview
Noun Key vocab: noun, person, place, thing, common noun, proper noun 🌙 Examples: Maryam, Makkah, Ramadan, patience (sabr)
3

Pronouns

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can replace nouns with the correct pronouns.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: Review nouns from last meeting
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is a Pronoun? Subject & object pronouns (I, you, she, he, we, they, it)
30 – 45 min
Activity: Replace the noun with a pronoun — sentence substitution game
45 – 55 min
Practice: Pronoun matching worksheet
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Pronoun Key vocab: pronoun, subject, object, replace 🌙 "Fatimah loves reading the Quran. She reads every morning."
4

Adjectives

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can describe nouns using adjectives.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: "Describe your prayer mat in 3 words"
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is an Adjective? Describing size, color, number, feeling
30 – 45 min
Activity: Describe the picture — students use adjectives to describe illustrated scenes
45 – 55 min
Practice: Add an adjective to each sentence
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Adjective Key vocab: adjective, describe, beautiful, kind, big, small 🌙 "The kind sister gave food to the poor." / "She wore a white hijab."
5

Verbs

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can identify action verbs and linking verbs in sentences.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: Act out a verb (charades-style)
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is a Verb? Action verbs vs. linking verbs; simple present tense
30 – 45 min
Activity: Match the subject with the correct verb
45 – 55 min
Practice: Write 3 sentences about your daily routine using verbs
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Verb Key vocab: verb, action, state, present tense, daily routine 🌙 "She reads the Quran every morning." / "We pray five times a day."
6

Review — Noun, Pronoun, Adjective, Verb

Review Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can identify and use all four parts of speech in context.
0 – 10 min
Quick quiz: Identify the part of speech (oral, no pressure)
10 – 30 min
Review Lesson: Summary of Noun, Pronoun, Adjective, Verb with examples
30 – 50 min
Group Activity: Build sentences together using all 4 parts of speech
50 – 58 min
Q&A and clarification of any confusion
58 – 60 min
Closing: What to expect in the exam
Review All 4 parts covered in integrated practice
7

Extra Practice & Exam Preparation

Exam Prep · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students feel confident and ready for the Session 1 exam.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: Fun vocabulary game
10 – 30 min
Guided practice: Exam-style questions with teacher walkthrough
30 – 50 min
Independent practice: Mini mock exam (ungraded)
50 – 58 min
Review answers together as a class
58 – 60 min
Pep talk + closing du'a
Exam Prep 🌙 Close with du'a for ease in learning
8

Session 1 Exam

Assessment · 60 minutes · Passing score: 60/100

▾
Goal: Assess understanding of Noun, Pronoun, Adjective, and Verb.
Section A
Identify the part of speech (underline / label) — 30 pts
Section B
Fill in the blank — 30 pts
Section C
Write your own sentences — 30 pts
Bonus
Islamic vocabulary challenge — 10 pts
Total: 100 pts Passing: 60 / 100 🌙 Students below 60 receive remedial support

10

Adverbs

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can identify adverbs and understand what they modify.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: "How do you pray? Describe it with one word."
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is an Adverb? Modifying verbs, adjectives, other adverbs; adverbs of manner, time, frequency
30 – 45 min
Activity: Adverb transformation — turn an adjective into an adverb using -ly
45 – 55 min
Practice: Rewrite sentences using adverbs
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Adverb Key vocab: adverb, quickly, slowly, always, never, very, daily 🌙 "She quietly recited the Quran." / "We always greet with salam."
11

Prepositions

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can use prepositions of place and time correctly.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: "Where is your Quran right now?"
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is a Preposition? In, on, at, under, beside, between, after, before
30 – 45 min
Activity: Describe the picture — where are the objects?
45 – 55 min
Practice: Fill in the blank with the correct preposition
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Preposition Key vocab: in, on, at, beside, between, before, after 🌙 "The prayer mat is on the floor." / "Iftar is after Maghrib."
12

Conjunctions

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can connect ideas using coordinating conjunctions (FANBOYS).
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: "Tell me two things you did today using the word 'and'."
10 – 30 min
Lesson: What is a Conjunction? FANBOYS — For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So
30 – 45 min
Activity: Combine two sentences into one using a conjunction
45 – 55 min
Practice: Choose the correct conjunction to complete each sentence
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Conjunction Key vocab: FANBOYS — and, but, or, so, for, nor, yet 🌙 "She wanted to fast, but she was sick." / "I read Quran and made du'a."
13

Interjections

Study Session · 60 minutes

▾
Learning Goal: Students can use interjections to express emotions naturally.
0 – 10 min
Warm-up: React to a funny/surprising picture with one word
10 – 25 min
Lesson: What is an Interjection? Expressing surprise, happiness, pain, hesitation
25 – 40 min
Activity: Match the interjection to the emotion or situation
40 – 55 min
Practice: Write a short dialogue using at least 2 interjections
55 – 60 min
Closing: Recap
Interjection Key vocab: wow, oops, oh, hurray, ouch, hmm 🌙 Islamic expressions: MashaAllah! Alhamdulillah! Astaghfirullah! SubhanAllah!

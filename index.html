About This Course
Course Description

English Basics 101 is a beginner-level course designed for Muslimahs who are just starting their English learning journey. This course focuses on understanding the 8 Parts of Speech — the essential building blocks of the English language — through clear explanations, relatable Islamic examples, and fun practice activities.


By the end of this course, students will be able to identify and use nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections in simple everyday sentences.

✦
What Students Will Learn
Learning Objectives
  • 1 Name and define all 8 parts of speech in English
  • 2 Identify each part of speech in a given sentence
  • 3 Use each part of speech correctly in simple sentences
  • 4 Build confidence in basic English communication using Islamic contexts

Etiquette
Classroom Rules
Opening & Closing Start every class with Bismillah and close with Alhamdulillah.
Respect Be respectful and supportive to all classmates at all times.
Presence Cameras on during online sessions when possible.
Questions Ask freely — there are no silly questions here!
Practice Complete activities before the next meeting.
Islamic Expressions Use MashaAllah, InshaAllah, JazakAllah Khair naturally in class.
